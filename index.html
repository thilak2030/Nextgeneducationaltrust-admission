Admission Requirements
Prepare these documents and check eligibility before applying

📄 Required Documents

  • Birth certificate (original + photocopy)
  • Previous school mark sheets / report cards
  • Transfer Certificate (TC) from last school
  • Aadhaar card / Government ID proof
  • Passport-size photographs (4 copies)
  • Address proof (utility bill / rental agreement)
  • Community / caste certificate (if applicable)
  • Completed application form

✅ Eligibility Criteria

  • Grade 1: Age 6 years as of June 1
  • Grades 2–8: Passed previous grade with min. 50%
  • Grade 9: Min. 55% in Grade 8 final exam
  • Grade 11 Science: Min. 65% in Grade 10, Science
  • Grade 11 Commerce: Min. 55% in Grade 10
  • Grade 11 Humanities: Min. 50% in Grade 10
  • No pending dues at previous institution
  • Good conduct certificate from previous school

💳 Fee Structure

  • Application Fee: ₹500 (non-refundable)
  • Admission Fee: ₹5,000 (one-time)
  • Tuition Fee (Primary): ₹3,500/month
  • Tuition Fee (Secondary): ₹4,500/month
  • Tuition Fee (Sr. Secondary): ₹5,500/month
  • Annual Charges: ₹8,000/year
  • Transportation (optional): ₹1,200/month
  • Scholarships available — ask admissions office

Frequently Asked Questions
Answers to the most common queries from students and parents
The deadline for submitting your completed application is March 31, 2025. Late applications may not be considered, so we strongly encourage families to apply well in advance.
Grades 1–3 require only an informal interaction. Grades 4 and above require a written assessment in English and Mathematics. Grade 11 applicants also attend a subject-specific interview.
Mid-year admissions are considered on a seat-availability basis. Contact the admissions office with your child's Transfer Certificate and latest mark sheet to begin the process.
Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships (up to 50% fee waiver) and need-based concessions. Apply for a scholarship at the time of admission and submit supporting income documents.
Results are published on the online portal on May 10. You will also receive an SMS and email notification. Log in with your application number to view the offer letter.
Seats not confirmed by May 31 may be offered to waitlisted students. Contact the admissions office immediately if you face difficulties — we do consider extensions in exceptional cases.
